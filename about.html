What Drink Am I?

"What Drink Am I?" is a fun and interactive quiz that helps you find out which drink best matches your personality based on the MBTI framework.
Inspired by the classic personality framework from Isabel Myers and Katharine Briggs, our test helps you discover your true beverage in just 12 questions! Are you smooth like a latte, bubbly like soda, or serene like herbal tea? By the end, you'll sip on some insights about your ultimate drink persona! Cheers to finding out what drink you really are!
